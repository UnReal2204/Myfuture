WELCOME TO WHO WANTS TO BE A MILLIONAIRE!


HERE ARE THE RULES OF THE GAME:

(i)There are 15questions that you have to answer in order to gain the full prize money which is $100000. Each question has 4 options and you have to choose one.
(ii)For every correct answer the prize money increases. You start at $0.
(iii)If you give an incorrect answer, it's Game Over!
(iv)You have three lifelines, namely 50/50, swap question or phone a friend.
-50/50 means that 50% of the answers will be eliminated, then you have to choose from the remaining two.
-Swap question means you can change the question.
-Phone a friend will choose a random answer for you, then you have to decide if you will go ahead with it or not.
(v)Once you choose a lifeline, it is eliminated hence you cannot choose it again.



PRESS PLAY IF YOU WANT TO CONTINUE.
